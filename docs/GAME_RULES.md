# Three Card - Game Rules

## Overview
Three Card is a multiplayer card game for 2-4 players using a standard 52-card deck. The goal is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards.

## Setup

### Card Distribution (per player)
1. **3 cards face down** - Cannot be seen until hand and face-up cards are gone
2. **3 cards face up** - Visible to all players
3. **3 cards in hand** - Hidden from other players

### Starting the Game
- Dealer is chosen randomly
- Cards are dealt to all players
- Remaining cards form the draw deck
- Player with lowest face-up card (or random) goes first

## Player Turn

### Playing Cards
1. Player must play **1 or more cards** of the **same value**
2. Card(s) played must be **equal to or higher** than the top card on the pile
3. If player has fewer than 3 cards in hand, draw from deck until they have 3
4. Cards are played to a central pile

### When You Can't Play
- If you cannot play a card equal to or higher (and no special cards)
- You must **pick up the entire pile** into your hand
- Your turn ends

## Special Cards

### 2 Card - Reset
- Can be played on **any card**
- **Resets** the pile (next player can play any card)
- Player who played the 2 gets **another turn**

### 10 Card - Blow Up
- Can be played on **any card**
- **Discards** the entire pile (pile is removed from game)
- Player who played the 10 gets **another turn**

### Four of a Kind - Blow Up
- If **4 cards of the same value** are on top of the pile
- Can be completed by one player or multiple players across turns
- **Discards** the entire pile
- Last player to complete it gets **another turn**

## Card Progression

### Playing from Hand
- Play from your hand cards normally
- Draw from deck to maintain 3 cards in hand
- Once deck is empty, no more drawing

### Playing Face-Up Cards
- Can only play face-up cards when **hand is empty**
- Treat face-up cards like hand cards
- Can play any face-up card if it's valid
- If face-up card is **lower** than pile top:
  - Must pick up the pile
  - Must also pick up **one face-up card** of your choice (into hand)

### Playing Face-Down Cards
- Can only play when **hand AND face-up cards are gone**
- Play **blindly** (don't know what card it is)
- If card is valid, it plays normally
- If card is invalid, pick up pile + the face-down card

## Winning

### Victory Condition
- First player to play all cards (hand, face-up, and face-down)
- Player must have **0 cards total**

### Game Continues
- Remaining players continue until all players finish
- Rankings: 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th place

## Card Ranking (Low to High)
3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, J, Q, K, A

**Special:** 2 (reset), 10 (blow up)

## Strategy Tips
- Save 2s and 10s for when you need them
- Try to complete four-of-a-kind when possible
- Keep track of face-up cards to plan ahead
- Face-down cards are pure luck!

## Example Turn Sequence

1. **Player A** plays two 7s (has three 7s, could play all)
2. **Player B** plays one 9
3. **Player C** plays four Queens → **BLOW UP** → pile discarded, Player C goes again
4. **Player C** plays a 4 (pile is empty after blow up)
5. **Player D** can't play, picks up pile
6. **Player A's** turn again...
